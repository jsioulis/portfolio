About

My name is John Sioulis

I'm currently 20 years old and if you want me to wrap this up right here, get to know that i'm super-interested in everything that involves technology and computers.

Over the last years, technology is evolving at an incredible rate. Right now, people use personal computers, laptops, tablets, smartphones etc. almost all the time during the day. It has become something that we all have incorporated in our lives and one of the reasons of why this is happening is -- the Internet!!

So, who doesn't want to have a window looking at the whole world right in his palms? That's right..

Having said all that, it's time to tell you about what i do. Web-designers - developers help make what i earlier said come true, as we are responsible for bringing this world to your hands. But it doesn't stop there. Everything moves on the web so, is it a personal website about your local business or even a whole branding plan of a company, market, movement etc. i am here to help!!

Personally i prefer building responsive websites, since they give a universal feeling that binds the desktop with the mobile world. My CMS of choice is Drupal. I have been using that platform for many years now and I have also contributed to the Drupal community, designing theme templates as well as developing modules, enhancing the platform.
